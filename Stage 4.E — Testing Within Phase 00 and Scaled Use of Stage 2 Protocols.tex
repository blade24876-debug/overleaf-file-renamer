\subsubsection*{Validity posture explicit}
\phantomsection

\begin{itemize}
\item Tests are tagged \textbf{VALID} or \textbf{INVALID} only (Stage 1.F). Any legacy term "Compromised" is treated as \textbf{INVALID} for gate decisions.
\item Week 16 is the exit-gate evaluation window. Week 12 is confirmatory only and cannot substitute for a missed/invalid Week-16 gate.
\item Underwater remains out of scope for Phase 00 gate credit:
  \begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{C14} / \textbf{MET-2B-025}; \textbf{MET-2B-026} are not scheduled in Phase 00 under any circumstance.
  \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
