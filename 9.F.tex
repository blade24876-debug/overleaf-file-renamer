\section*{9.F.3 Closing Statement}
\phantomsection
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{9.F.3 Closing Statement}

This is a long-horizon, high-demand program. Honest reporting and complete logging are mandatory because gates rely on admissible evidence only. Stop immediately for red-flag symptoms and for pain that alters mechanics, and report to the coach without delay. Do not attempt to “push through” safety signals or to salvage gate attempts under compromised conditions; reslot discipline preserves safety and validity.
